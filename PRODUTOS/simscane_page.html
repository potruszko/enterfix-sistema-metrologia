Dimensional Analysis to Evaluate Shaft Alignment Using K-SCAN Magic II

A precise 3D inspection solution to correct shaft misalignment and improve the reliability of large wood-processing machinery.

11 Dec, 2025

From Challenge to Solution: Precision 3D Inspection in Aluminum Manufacturing

In aluminum extrusion manufacturing, precision and efficiency are critical. Dajcor Aluminum faced challenges inspecting large, intricate parts with strict tolerances. By adopting SCANOLOGY’s TrackScan Sharp-E 3D scanning system paired with the i-Probe 500, the company achieved faster, more accurate, and more flexible quality inspections—streamlining operations and enhancing productivity across its facilities.
