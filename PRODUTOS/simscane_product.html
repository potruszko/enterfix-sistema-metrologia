Intelligent, Wireless and Palm-Sized 3D Scanner
Precision in Your Palm
Watch video Buy Now
The SIMSCAN-E is an intelligent, wireless, and palm-sized 3D scanner that combines a lightweight design with exceptional performance. Featuring advanced edge computing and wireless data transfer, it sets a new standard for flexible, wireless and free 3D scanning.
Wireless Data Transfer
Blue Laser Lines 81
630,000 measurements/s
203mm x 80mm x 44mm
With robust algorithm and high-definition industrial cameras, SIMSCAN-E can capture 3D data with remarkable precision and efficiency, measuring up to 6.3 million measurements/s. It boasts three scanning modes, ultra-fast, hyperfine, and deep hole. This versatility allows it to handle a wide range of tasks effortlessly, from scanning in tight spaces to measuring complex structures.
Fast, Smooth, and Efficient
High Measurement Rate
Thanks to its advanced algorithm, SIMSCAN-E delivers a high measurement rate of 6.3 million measurements/s.
Smooth Scanning
Coupled with 81 blue laser lines and a 180-FPS frame rate, it ensures efficient and smooth scanning experiences.
Diverse Operating Modes
63 blue laser lines for efficient scanning to evaluate the geometry and dimensions of parts quickly.
17 parallel blue laser lines for swift detail capturing of intricate features and complex structures.
1 blue laser line for measuring deep holes and hard-to-reach areas.
Ultra-fast scanning
Hyperfine scanning
Deep hole scanning
Compact, Wireless, and Effortless
SIMSCAN-E combines a compact 203 × 80 × 44 mm full-metal design, lightweight 600 g build, and built-in wireless capabilities to deliver true one-handed portability.
Its streamlined form and cable-free setup make scanning easy and efficient—whether you're on the shopfloor, at height, or in remote locations.
Robust Data Processing
Sophisticated edge computing and wireless data transfer ensure smooth scanning in any environment.
Long-Lasting Power
Dual batteries allow hot-swapping for continuous operation.
Adaptable Use
Switch easily between wired and wireless modes with a detachable charging base.
Exceptional Detail Capture
Powered by its innovative algorithm and aerospace-grade components, its measurement accuracy is up to 0.020 mm, meeting the demands of industrial-grade high-precision 3D scanning and measurement. From product development to inspection, we help users track and record the data during the entire product lifecycle.
Industrial cameras

Image enhancement algorithm

Sub-pixel feature extraction algorithm

Excellent for Narrow Spaces
Flexible and lightweight, this portable 3D scanner supports instant scanning in various environments. Its short-distance camera design, with a steep view angle, greatly enhances the ability to scan hidden areas such as gaps, slots, deep holes, and channels. This significantly improves data integrity, providing more complete 3D data for further design, inspection, analysis, and more.
