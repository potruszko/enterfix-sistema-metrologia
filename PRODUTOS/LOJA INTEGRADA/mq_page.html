About 3DeVOK MQ 3D Scanner

The 3DeVOK MQ 3D Scanner, a versatile 3d scanner for industrial use, combines 22-line infrared laser and infrared speckle technology with dual FoV for professional applications. Wireless, intuitive, and efficient, MQ enables users to capture highly accurate 3D data anytime, anywhere. Ideal for 3D printing, creative design, cultural heritage preservation, reverse engineering, art, medical rehabilitation, and research, it is the ultimate productivity partner for design studios, 3D printing farms, and small to medium-sized businesses.

What is the scanning speed of 3DeVOK MT?

With marker point alignment, the maximum speed can reach 70 FPS. With hybrid alignment, it can reach up to 30 FPS. The higher the scanning frame rate, the more data features are captured in a single frame, making the alignment smoother and scanning speed faster.

What is the optimal scanning distance of 3DeVOK MT?

The scanning distance of the 3DeVok MT varies depending on the light source: it is 300mm for structured light and infrared laser, and 210mm for blue laser.
